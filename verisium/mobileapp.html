VERISIUM uses Quorum™ blockchain - an enterprise-ready distributed ledger and a smart contract platform developed by J.P.Morgan

How blockchain work

  • As each transaction occurs, it’s put into a block.

  • Each block is connected to the one before and after it.

  • Transactions are blocked together.

  • Each block is added to the next in an irreversible chain.

  • To form a block, all the parties should come to a consensus on transactions

What information is stored in blockchain

  • CHIPS’ UID & TID storage

  • ASSOCIATION of product codes with UID/TIDs

  • LIST of original items with their statuses

Distributed

It works as a system of record that is shared among participants of the business network, eliminating the need to reconcile disparate ledgers.

Permissioned

Each member of the network has access rights so that confdential information is shared on a need-to-know basis.

Secured

Consensus is required from all network members and all validated transactions are permanently recorded. No one, not even a system administrator, can delete information.

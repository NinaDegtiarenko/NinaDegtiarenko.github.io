Anti–counterfeit system

based on RFID and Blockchain technology

Counterfeiting destroys brand equity and leads to lost sales

We believe that counterfeiting is a disease. Like many diseases, it can be dealt with – once and for all. With Verisium, counterfeiters have no chance to present their fakes as genuine products. Verisium is built in a way that makes forging a tag next to IMPOSSIBLE and economically IMPRACTICAL. Product authenticity is verified at many stages, culminating in blockchain entry verification. Even if a perfect clone of a chip is made, it is recognized as a fake immediately.

Communicate to your customers through products

Verisium Platform allows you to build a fully custom, media-rich product card that users see when they scan the chip. Implement breathtaking digital scenarios – while your real-world product stays in focus

When the brand-to-user connection is made, customer gets to know you better – and vice versa. With Verisium you can measure not only sales, as most brands do, but also pre- and post-sale product interactions. You also get to know your users – what their devices are, what other products from you and other brands they are interested in and consider buying. With geo-reports, you can easily see the how your products take over the world.
